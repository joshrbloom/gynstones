What We Do

Real-time feedback for resident growth

GynStones connects medical residents with faculty evaluators through a streamlined digital workflow. After each clinical encounter, faculty are automatically notified and can submit structured assessments — making feedback timely, consistent, and actionable.

Our Approach

Built on international standards

Our evaluation framework is grounded in Entrustable Professional Activities (EPAs) aligned with international OB/GYN competency standards. Each assessment captures both technical skill and professional behavior, building a comprehensive picture of resident progress over time.
